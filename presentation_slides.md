## Slide 2: The Problem
**Why do fans switch off?**
*   **Static Data**: Traditional scorecards are boring and don't tell the "story" of the match.
*   **Delayed Insights**: Commentary often arrives after the momentum has already shifted.
*   **Low Engagement**: Fans have no real-time way to influence or interact with the analytical narrative.

---

## Slide 3: The Solution
**Introducing CricStat AI**
*   A **High-Fidelity AI Dashboard** that transforms raw match data into live tactical strategies.
*   Bridges the gap between **Fans**, **AI**, and **Real-time Analytics**.
*   Built on Google Cloud for extreme low latency and scalability.

---

## Slide 4: Key Feature: Tension Engine
**Measuring the Unmeasurable**
*   **Real-time Volatility**: Proprietary algorithm calculates "Match Tension" (1-10) based on ball-by-ball pressure.
*   **Predictive Alerts**: Visual pulses and "Flash Danger" UI warn fans of an impending wicket.
*   **Data-Driven**: Every run, dot, and wicket shifts the momentum bar instantly.

---

## Slide 5: Key Feature: Gemini Strat-Insights
**Your Personal AI Coach**
*   **Contextual Analysis**: Gemini 1.5 Pro analyzes the match state to provide deep tactical cards.
*   **AI Voice Commentary**: Multi-modal feedback using Browser Speech API for accessible, real-time commentary.
*   **Beyond Stats**: Tells you *why* a wicket fell and *how* the next batter should guard their stumps.

---

## Slide 6: Key Feature: Community Oracle
**The Pulse of the Match**
*   **Live Aggregation**: Real-time poll system integrated directly into the analytics flow.
*   **Sentiment vs. AI**: Compare fan predictions with AI Win Probabilities.
*   **Interactive Design**: Material Design 3 interactive bars that update atomically on every vote.

## Slide 7: Technical Architecture
**The "Extreme Coders" Stack**
*   **Frontend**: Angular 17+ (Reactive & Modular)
*   **Intelligence**: Gemini AI (Vertex API) for Tactical Generation.
*   **Real-time DB**: Firebase Firestore for atomic voting and score sync.
*   **Hosting**: Firebase Global Edge Hosting.
*   **Voice**: Web Speech API for multimodal delivery.
